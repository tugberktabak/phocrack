Introduction

PhoCrack is an open-source project. We are committed to protecting your privacy and minimizing data collection. The software operates locally on your device and does not automatically transmit personal information to external servers.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

Any data processed by PhoCrack is stored locally on your device. We implement reasonable security measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

Our project is hosted on GitHub, which has its own privacy policy. When you interact with our repository or submit issues, GitHub's privacy policy applies to that interaction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of any data collection practices

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us
